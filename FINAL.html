CSU APPLICATION

The CSU application requires a series of steps in order to submit your favorite campuses.The application requires you to fill out different topics. These topics are your personal information, academic history, supporting information and program materials.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

The personal information gives the school a sence of your background information. This type of information ask for stuff such as your SSN, citicenship status, ethnicy, and your permission to use that information for academic purposes. It also offers an academic oportunity program.
